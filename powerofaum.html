Context

Monetization often conflicts with trust.

Promotion changes behavior

When content becomes transactional, audiences often perceive a shift in intent.

Value-sharing is hard to measure

Most systems do not account for indirect or long-term influence across networks.

Attribution is usually limited

Credit is typically assigned only to the final step in a transaction rather than the broader chain of introduction.

Existing tools prioritize conversion

Many platforms are optimized for sales activity rather than informational or trust-based distribution.

Method

Designed around attribution, not persuasion.

PowerOfAum is structured as background infrastructure. It records how value moves through a network when something useful is shared voluntarily.

01

A resource is shared

A participant shares access to a product or service they consider useful.

02

Recipients decide independently

There is no requirement to purchase or promote. Engagement remains optional.

03

Sharing may continue organically

If recipients find value, they may choose to pass it along further.

04

Attribution is recorded

When transactions occur, attribution follows the path of introduction.
